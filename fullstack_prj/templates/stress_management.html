Stress Management

Simple actions can help you slow down and feel more in control.

Stress Management Tips

  • Take slow breaths: inhale for 4 seconds and exhale for 6 seconds.
  • Take a short break from screens or work when you feel overwhelmed.
  • Go for a short walk or stretch gently to release tension.
  • Write down what is worrying you and choose one small next step.
  • Talk to someone you trust when stress feels difficult to manage alone.
  • Keep a regular sleep routine and drink enough water.
Quick reset: relax your shoulders, unclench your jaw, take five slow breaths, and focus on one task at a time.

If stress feels overwhelming or you feel unsafe, contact a trusted person or local professional/emergency support promptly.
